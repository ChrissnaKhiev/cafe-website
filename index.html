About Us - The Cats Cottage

Our Cat-ffeinated Tale

At The Cats Cottage, our story begins with a deep love for cats and an unending passion for coffee and tea. Sahra, the founder, shares this love and was inspired by her own feline companion, Luna, to create a space where people could find comfort, relaxation, and the perfect blend of caffeine and cat-inspired companionship.

A Purr-fect Experience

Our mission is simple: we're dedicated to ensuring you enjoy every moment you spend at The Cats Cottage. We want you to savor your treats, sip your coffee, and bask in the cozy vibes of our felineinspired shop.

The Ultimate Cat-Coffee Experience

We've poured our hearts into creating the ultimate cat-coffee experience for you. Our café is adorned with whimsical cat spots onthe walls, and you can savor cat-shaped cookies and fish-shaped waffles (because cats do adore fish, after all). Explore our menu with cat-inspired names like the "Caramel Biscat" and "Strays."

Meet the Team

As of now, The Cats Cottage is run by Sahra and her partner, Carlos. We're passionate about delivering the perfect cup of coffee and providing you with a warm, welcoming atmosphere.

Cats, Coffee, and Chill Vibes

When you step into The Cats Cottage, you'll be greeted by the soothing melodies of a vinyl record player, spinning chill tunes throughout the day. Our cozy couches are here for you to unwind, sip your coffee, and relax in the embrace of our cat-themed decor.

A Glimpse into the Future

The Cats Cottage is more than just a coffee shop; it's a community. We have exciting plans for the future, including trivia nights, movienights, and other special events on select Fridays each month. Join us in building lasting memories in the heart of Memphis. At The Cats Cottage, we're more than a coffee shop – we're a place where cat lovers and coffee aficionados come together to celebrate life's simple pleasures. Join us, and let's create purr-fect moments, one cup of coffee and one contented cat at a time. Contact Us to find out more or simply drop by and experience The Cats Cottage for yourself.
